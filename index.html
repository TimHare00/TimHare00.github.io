Experience

JCB

Work Experience

I worked at JCB’s experimental division; the electrification team introduced me to their CAD package NX, as well as their engineering database platform, Teamcenter. Using this, I was able to concept design new battery technology into a mini excavator already in production. The concept involved introducing new components into the machine and packaging them to optimise their performance. The aim of this series 1 electrification concept was to reduce costs onto the machine by not making changes to the existing chassis or hydraulic system. While packaging the new electrical components in the existing diesel chassis, I created a fit-for-purpose sheet metal bracket. It housed the hydraulic cooling pack along with ventilation fans. The flange tool enabled me to mount the inverter and DC-DC converter in the airflow created. With support I created a drafting of this design in accordance to JCB’s standards while making this part as cost effective and simple as possible for the manufacturer.
